Core Subjects

  1. Financial Accounting:

    Understanding the principles of accounting and preparing financial statements.

  2. Business Law:

    Covers legal aspects of business, including contracts and regulatory compliance.

  3. Economics:

    Introduction to micro and macroeconomic principles affecting business decisions.

  4. Business Statistics:

    Application of statistical methods to business data for analysis and decision-making.

  5. Financial Management:

    Focuses on managing finances, investments, and financial planning.

  6. Marketing Management:

    Principles and strategies for effective marketing in business contexts.

  7. Human Resource Management:

    Understanding the management of people within organizations.
