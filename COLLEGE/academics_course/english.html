BA English

Core Subjects

  1. History of English Literature:

    Covers major literary movements and influential authors from different periods.

  2. Indian Writing in English:

    Explores works by Indian authors writing in English, examining themes and cultural contexts.

  3. Shakespeare:

    Focuses on the comedies and tragedies of William Shakespeare, analyzing their impact on literature.

  4. English Poetry and Prose:

    Studies various forms of poetry and prose from different eras, including significant works and authors.

  5. Literary Criticism:

    Introduces various critical approaches to literature, enhancing analytical skills.
